Collages

Digital collages



Born out of my interest in graphic design, I find collages to be a very dramatic and effective art type, when done well. It mirrors reality and it transcends it, all by using real-life images. I find it very effective for expressing complex feelings, as I sometimes feel as if I can see my emotions in real time in certain places, and not just feel them. In this particular case, I want to have a very specific medium. You could say that painting and drawing could work quite well here, but I find it difficult to express deeply abstract concepts through them, because I am neither skilled nor creative enough (yet), and I feel that the greatest impact is brought about by the use of raw elements from life around me, rather than through the movements of my hands.


An der Spüle

An der Spüle (At the sink) is my first collage, representing the general charming disconfort I go thorugh every day in my less than 9m² room. As time goes by, I can only hope to get out of here to a better place, but the lurking melancholy already sets in, in the anticipation of what the lusophones world calls "saudade", "dor" in Romanian. It's almost as if I see this image in my imagination and in the mirror of my mind rather than with my eyes. The bottle of cyanide is there to remind me of my fascination with nostalgia, misery and gloominess, directly contradicting my wish for social ascention. It equally points out the sensation of finding myslef somewhere between life and death, outside of reality, whenever I spend too much time in my room.
