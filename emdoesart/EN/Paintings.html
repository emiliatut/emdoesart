Paintings

Tempera, watercolour



Painting was my first ever art-related hobby and I remember displaying interest in it ever since I was in kindergarten. It finally properly installed itself during middleschool. My teacher at the time was very passionate about what he was doing, he appreciated my efforts and saw the potential in me.

I bought a lot of art supplies those years. Stil haven't used them all... Damn you, highschool!

In spite of all this, painting is the medium I feel least confident in.

My love for miniature continues, although a majority of my works so far have been on big surfaces.
